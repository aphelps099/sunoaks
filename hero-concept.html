Design Notes

What changed and why

Current Hero
  • Large yellow sun circle with spinning rays
  • Three circular photo cutouts
  • Headline shares space with imagery
  • Two stats + CTA in bottom bar
  • Multiple competing focal points
This Concept
  • Typography IS the hero — nothing competes
  • Headline at ~9vw, three lines, owns the viewport
  • Single yellow rule as color punctuation
  • Four-column stat bar anchored at bottom
  • Staggered text reveal animation
Collins-inspired principle: "Ubiquity = invisibility. What becomes common, we stop seeing." This hero strips away decorative elements so the brand statement hits with authority. The yellow sun circle and photo cluster can still appear further down the page — they don't need to compete with the opening statement.
