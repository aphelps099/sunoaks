01
Jan 1 – 31

New Year, New You

January enrollment push—capture resolution-makers while motivation is high.

Offer
  • $0 Initiation Fee
  • First month 50% off
  • Free PT session included
  • 7-day trial for referrals
Audience
  • Lapsed leads (6–12 mo)
  • Former members
  • Referral list
  • Local community (geo)
Channels
Email Social In-Club Website
  • Facebook/Instagram Ads
  • Google Search (brand + gym)
Timeline
Wk 1 Teaser emails + social countdown
Wk 2 Full campaign launch, ads live
Wk 3 Testimonial push + urgency
Wk 4 "Last chance" + retargeting
02
May 15 – Jun 30

Summer Splash

Family & aquatics focus—pools, camps, fun. Capture families before summer plans lock in.

Offer
  • Family membership discount
  • Free summer camp week
  • Pool pass bundle
  • Swim lesson package deal
Audience
  • Families w/ kids (5–15)
  • Current members (upsell)
  • School newsletter lists
  • Local parent groups
Channels
Email Social In-Club Website
  • School flyers & partnerships
  • Facebook Groups (local moms)
Timeline
Wk 1 "Summer is coming" teaser
Wk 2–3 Camp registration push
Wk 4–5 Pool & swim lesson focus
Wk 6 Final push + early bird ends
03
Sep 1 – 30

Fall Back Into Fitness

Back-to-routine season—recapture summer drop-offs and empty nesters.

Offer
  • Rejoin with $0 fee
  • 3-month commitment special
  • Free class pack (5 classes)
  • Buddy pass for a friend
Audience
  • Cancelled members (3–12 mo)
  • Summer freeze accounts
  • Trial non-converters
  • Empty nesters
Channels
Email Social In-Club Website
  • Direct mail (lapsed VIPs)
  • Retargeting ads
Timeline
Wk 1 "We miss you" personalized email
Wk 2 New class schedule highlight
Wk 3 Member success stories
Wk 4 "Ends soon" final push
