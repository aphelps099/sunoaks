05 Best Practices

Make every email count

Subject Lines

Keep them under 50 characters. Use the member's name when appropriate. Create curiosity without clickbait. Test two versions when possible.

Send Times

Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9am or 7-9pm perform best. Avoid Mondays (inbox overwhelm) and Fridays (weekend mode). Test your own audience.

Personalization

Always use first name. Reference their membership type when relevant. Segment by interest (tennis, aquatics, group fitness) when possible.

One CTA Per Email

Every email should have one clear action. Multiple CTAs dilute focus. Make buttons obvious and action-oriented ("Book Now" not "Learn More").
