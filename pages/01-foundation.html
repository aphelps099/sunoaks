Our Story

The Beginning

In 1960, Redding was a small Northern California city searching for its identity. The lumber industry was slowing, the population was growing, and families needed places to gather—not just to exercise, but to belong.

Sun Oaks began as a vision: six acres of oak-studded land that could become the community's backyard. A place where kids could learn to swim on summer mornings, where parents could find an hour of peace in the gym, where neighbors could become friends over a game of tennis.

"We didn't set out to build a fitness center. We set out to build a place where Redding could find itself."

Sixty-four years later, that vision hasn't changed—only deepened. Three generations of families have grown up within these walls. The morning lap swimmers know each other by name. The after-school tennis kids become the adult league players. The community we imagined has become the community we are.

Today, Sun Oaks serves thousands of members across seven distinct programs: fitness, tennis, pickleball, aquatics, kids, spa, and events. But the numbers have never been the point. The point is what happens between those numbers—the conversations in the locker room, the high-fives after a tough class, the generations that learn to love movement together.

1.2

Our Timeline

1960

The Beginning

Sun Oaks opens on six acres of oak-studded land with a single pool, two tennis courts, and a dream of building community.

1975

Fitness Expansion

The first dedicated fitness center opens, introducing Redding to group exercise classes and modern training equipment.

1988

Tennis Complex

Expansion to 10 tennis courts makes Sun Oaks the premier tennis destination in Northern California.

2001

Aquatics Center

Three-pool aquatics complex opens, featuring competition lanes, a family pool, and dedicated learn-to-swim facility.

2015

Full-Service Spa

Wellness expansion introduces massage, skincare, and recovery services—completing the mind-body offering.

2021

Pickleball Courts

Four dedicated pickleball courts answer the community's fastest-growing demand, bringing a new generation to Sun Oaks.

2024

Brand Evolution

Sun Oaks embarks on a comprehensive brand refresh, honoring 64 years of legacy while positioning for the next generation.

Core Values

What We Stand For
01
