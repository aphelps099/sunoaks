Brand Voice

How Sun Oaks speaks.

We're not a corporate gym chain. We're your neighbor. Our voice reflects 64 years of welcoming Redding families—warm, confident, and always real.

Voice Pillars

Three qualities define our voice

Every piece of communication should feel like it came from the same person—someone who genuinely cares about your wellbeing.

01

Warm

We speak like a trusted friend, not a faceless brand. Personal, inviting, and genuinely happy to see you. This isn't customer service—it's community.

Sounds like
  • We saved you a lane.
  • See you at 6am tomorrow?
  • Your kids are growing up here.
02

Confident

64 years of history gives us quiet confidence. We don't need to oversell—we let our facilities, programs, and community speak for themselves.

Sounds like
  • The only indoor courts in the North State.
  • Where Redding learns to swim.
  • Three generations. Same club.
03

Real

No gym-speak. No corporate jargon. No "crushing it" or "gains." We talk like normal people who happen to care about fitness and community.

Sounds like
  • It's hot. The pool is open.
  • Rain or shine, your swim never gets cancelled.
  • The 6am crew awaits.

Do say

  • "Your workout is waiting" — inviting, personal
  • "Join us" — inclusive, community-focused
  • "See you soon" — warm, assumes relationship
  • "Since 1960" — confident, heritage-driven
  • "Families like yours" — relatable, specific
  • "Your place" — ownership, belonging

Don't say

  • "Crush your goals" — too aggressive, gym-bro
  • "Act now!" — salesy, pressure tactics
  • "Dear valued member" — corporate, impersonal
  • "State-of-the-art facility" — generic, oversold
  • "Results guaranteed" — unrealistic, gimmicky
  • "Exclusive offer" — sounds like spam
Key Messages

Stories we tell

These core messages can be adapted across channels—from website copy to social posts to sales conversations.

Heritage

Three generations of Redding families have grown up here.

Since 1960, Sun Oaks has been the place where kids learn to swim, teens find their serve, and adults find their people. We're not just a gym—we're part of the community's story.

Belonging

More than a gym. This is your place.

The 6am swimmers know each other's names. The tennis regulars grab coffee after matches. The moms in water aerobics have been friends for decades. You're not joining a membership—you're joining a community.

Facilities

Everything you need, under one roof.

Three pools. Ten tennis courts (the only indoor courts in the North State). 70+ group classes per week. A full gym. Childcare. Spa services. Six acres of space for every way you want to move.

Value

One membership. Unlimited possibilities.

No add-on fees for classes. No extra charge for pools or courts. No hidden costs. Your membership includes everything—because that's how it should be.
