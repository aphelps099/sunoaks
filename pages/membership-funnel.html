Common questions

Can I try before I commit?
Absolutely. Stop by anytime for a free tour—no appointment needed. Our team will show you around and answer all your questions. We're here Monday-Saturday.
Is there a contract?
We offer both month-to-month and annual options. Annual memberships save you the enrollment fee and lock in your rate. Either way, we make it easy.
What if I need to pause my membership?
Life happens. We offer membership freezes for medical reasons, extended travel, or other circumstances. Just talk to our membership team—we'll work with you.
Are kids included in the family membership?
Yes! Family memberships include 2 adults and all children under 18 living in the same household. Kids have access to pools, Kids Club, youth programs, and more.
Can I bring a guest?
Members can bring guests for a small daily fee. New members get guest passes included in their first month—perfect for showing friends why you love Sun Oaks.
